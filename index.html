COVID-19 and the Future Survey Data

About

A graphical representation of the data collected from the COVID 19 and the Future Survey. This project is a colaborative effort of University of Illinois at Chicago, Arizona State University and The National Science Foundation.

The visualizations provide a detailed analisys on the effects on Employment, Productivity and Job changes due ro COVID 19.

Instructions:
  • Use the filters above the map to select the preferable population and category.
  • Interact by hovering and clicking on the map to see state specific data.
  • Use the graph below the map for an overall comparion of the changing trends.
  • Use the timeline on the right to analize monthly data and compare between states.
